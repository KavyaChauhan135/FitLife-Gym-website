Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FitLife

What are your operating hours?

We're open 24/7 to accommodate all schedules! Our facility is fully staffed during peak hours (6 AM - 10 PM), and members can access the gym at any time using their key fob.

Do you offer personal training sessions?

Yes! We offer personalized training sessions with our certified trainers. Sessions can be booked individually or as part of a package. Our trainers specialize in various areas including strength training, weight loss, and sports-specific conditioning.

What equipment and facilities do you have?

Our facility includes state-of-the-art cardio equipment, free weights, resistance machines, functional training areas, group exercise studios, and a dedicated stretching zone. We also have locker rooms with showers and towel service.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year. This service is free for medical reasons, and a small fee applies for other circumstances. Contact our membership team for details.

Do you offer group fitness classes?

Absolutely! We offer a wide variety of group classes including yoga, HIIT, spin, Zumba, and more. Classes are included in all membership levels, and our schedule is updated weekly to accommodate different fitness levels and preferences.
