Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a joining fee?

Yes, there is a one-time joining fee of $49 for all membership plans. However, we frequently run promotions where this fee is waived. Check our current offers for details.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year. A small monthly maintenance fee of $5 applies during the freeze period.

What is your cancellation policy?

All memberships require a 30-day written notice for cancellation. There are no cancellation fees if you've completed your minimum contract period (3 months for monthly plans).
